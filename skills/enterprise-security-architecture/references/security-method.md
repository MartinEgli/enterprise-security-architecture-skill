# Security Architecture Method

Use this sequence:

1. Identify business process and asset.
2. Identify data classification.
3. Identify actors, identities, and privileges.
4. Identify trust boundaries and integrations.
5. Identify threats and misuse cases.
6. Identify vulnerabilities or design weaknesses.
7. Map controls.
8. Assess residual risk.
9. Recommend approval, conditions, or rework.

## Risk Criteria

- likelihood
- impact
- exploitability
- exposure
- data sensitivity
- blast radius
- detectability
- reversibility
